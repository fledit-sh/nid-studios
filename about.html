02 — About Independent Trondheim, NO

One studio. One river.

We're a small web studio. We met in Trondheim, we live here, and we build websites the way we'd want to be built for: carefully.

From the
river Nid.

Nidelva runs through Trondheim, around the old town, past the cathedral, into the fjord. It's the line everyone draws when they're explaining where they live.

We took the name because we wanted a studio that felt local, slow-moving, and impossible to mistake for a marketing agency.
