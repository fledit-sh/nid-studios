03 — Method

Four stages.
No improvisation.

Stage 01

Discovery

We sit down — in person where possible — and pull the brief apart. Constraints, history, edge cases.

≈ 1 — 2 weeks
Stage 02

Proposal

A written plan. Scope, milestones, fixed price. If we can't agree on the page, we can't agree on the work.

≈ 1 week
Stage 03

Execution

Heads down, weekly check-ins, no ghost weeks. You see what we see; raw versions and all.

≈ 4 — 12 weeks
Stage 04

Delivery

Source files, documentation, a walkthrough, and a calendar invite ninety days out to check in on what we shipped.
